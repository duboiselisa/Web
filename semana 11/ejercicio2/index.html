THE MIND OF WATERCOLOR

Hello, my name is Steve Mitchell. Welcome to the Mind of Watercolor. I have been a professional designer and illustrator for 30+ years but now its time to change gears a bit and return to my fine art roots. This channel is for watercolor enthusiasts where I share tips and techniques in a quick, easy to digest fashion. I'll also do product reviews, offer challenges, look at other great artists' work and perhaps even have a few giveaways. Take a break with me and step into the Mind of Watercolor.

I intend to post a video every week when possible but this schedule is currently subject to interruptions by my "real job" :-)
